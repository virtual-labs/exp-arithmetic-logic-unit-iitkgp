Design of Arithmetic Logic Unit (Note 2)

INSTRUCTION

  1. Apply low voltage to M (mode control input) and \(C_{n}\).
  2. Apply \(V_{CC}\) and low level voltage to ground(GND).
  3. Apply low voltage to all the 4 select inputs (\(S_{3}\) \(S_{2}\) \(S_{1}\) \(S_{0}\)). Arithmetic operation (Note 1) will be performed.The output will be A minus 1
  4. Next,apply high voltage to \(S_{0}\) and apply low voltage to all other select inputs (\(S_{3}\) \(S_{2}\) \(S_{1}\)).The output will be AB minus 1.
  5. Now apply high voltage to \(S_{0}\) and low voltage to all other select inputs (\(S_{3}\) \(S_{2}\) \(S_{1}\) ).The output will be AB minus 1.
  6. Apply all the combinations to all the select inputs (\(S_{3}\) \(S_{2}\) \(S_{1}\) \(S_{0}\)) and check the output.
  7. Note: Red symbolize as Low (L), Green symbolize as High(H).
